About

Who I am

I'm a first-year secondary teacher at Arthur Phillip High School in Parramatta, NSW. I teach Technology and Multimedia to Years 7, 8, 9, and 10.

Before teaching, I worked in data analytics and GIS. I have a Master of Demography and a Bachelor of Science in Geographic Information Systems. Right now I'm finishing a Master of Secondary Teaching and a Computer Science degree.

What I care about

I build classroom tools that respect students' time. Every worksheet, slide deck, and tutorial I publish is something a 14-year-old would actually want to open.

  • NSW syllabus-aligned materials with real-world hooks
  • Three-tier differentiation on every resource (Support / Core / Extension)
  • Australian English, plain language, encouraging tone

Languages
