# 10 Multimedia · 2026 · Teaching Hub

Year 10 Industrial Technology — Multimedia. Class 10IMMYA, Arthur Phillip High School, Parramatta NSW.

Built by **[Ms Yunshu Gao](https://github.com/YunshuGao)** as a complete student-facing teaching hub for two terms of work:

- **Term 1 — App Design** (archive): [term1.html](term1.html)
- **Term 2 — Web Design** (22 lessons): [term2.html](term2.html)

🔗 **Live site:** https://yunshugao.github.io/10immya-2026/

## What's in here

- `index.html` — landing page with Term 1 / Term 2 navigation
- `term1.html` — Term 1 App Design archive
- `term2.html` — Term 2 Web Design main page with 22 lesson cards
- `lessons/l01.html` → `lessons/l22.html` — full lesson pages (Learning Intentions, Success Criteria, Do Now, I Do, We Do, You Do, Support/Core/Extension differentiation, Exit Ticket, Resources)
- `exemplars/` — working HTML/CSS files students can right-click → View Source on
- `exemplars/portfolio-complete/` — the finished 4-page portfolio students are building towards
- `assets/style.css` — the hub's stylesheet, itself a teaching artefact

## Syllabus alignment

NSW NESA Industrial Technology 7–10 Syllabus (2019), Multimedia focus area:

- **Term 1:** Multimedia 2 Specialist — Apps and Interactivity
- **Term 2:** Multimedia 1 Core — Web Design

Outcomes addressed: IND5-1 through IND5-10.
